# Quality Department

## Description
The Quality Department ensures all products and processes meet company standards, customer requirements, and regulatory compliance. We maintain ISO 9001 certification and oversee quality control, inspection, auditing, and continuous improvement initiatives throughout the organization.

## Responsibilities
- ISO 9001 quality management system maintenance
- Incoming material inspection and receiving quality
- In-process inspection and testing
- Final product inspection and test
- Non-conformance reporting and corrective action
- Supplier quality management
- Internal audit planning and execution
- Customer audit support and coordination
- Quality metrics tracking and reporting
- Process capability studies and analysis
- Root cause analysis and corrective/preventive actions
- Quality training and certification programs
